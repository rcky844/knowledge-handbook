#### Formulas
- **Equation**: $(x-h)^2 + (y-k)^2 = r^2$
- **General formula**: $x^2 + y^2 + Ax + By + C = 0$
- **Coordinates of centre**: $({-A\over2}, {-B\over2})$ or $(h, k)$
- **Radius**: $\sqrt{({-A\over2})^2 + ({-B\over2})^2 - C}$ = $\sqrt{h^2 + k^2 - C}$
