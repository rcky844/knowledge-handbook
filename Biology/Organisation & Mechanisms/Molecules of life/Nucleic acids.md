# Structure

| :----------------: | :-------------------------------------------------------: | :-----------------------------------------------------------: |
|   **Structure**    |                       Double helix                        |                         Single chain                          |
| **5-carbon-sugar** |                        Deoxyribose                        |                            Ribose                             |
