$\ce{- NH2}$
Amine group

> **Primary amide**
> ━━━━━━━━━━
> **Suffix**: -amine
> **General formula**: $\ce{R-NH2}$
> **Melting point & boiling point**: Higher than alkanes, but lower than alcohols and carboxylic acids
> **Density**: Less dense than water
> **Solubility**: Soluble in water (for no. of $\ce{C}$ < 4)
