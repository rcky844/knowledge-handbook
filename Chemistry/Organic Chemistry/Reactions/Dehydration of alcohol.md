# With conc. $\ce{H2SO4}$
![[h2so4_dehydration.webp|300]]

> alcohols → alkenes
> ━━━━━━━━━━
> **Reagent**: Conc. $\ce{H2SO4}$
> **Condition**: Under heating (180°C)

*Zaitzev’s Rule*:
The $\ce{H}$ atom of the $\ce{C}$ atom that has the least $\ce{H}$ atom attached is reacted.
